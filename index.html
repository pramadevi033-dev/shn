How fast can you get me a Schengen appointment?
+
Most clients receive a confirmed appointment within 1–5 business days. In urgent cases, we have secured slots within 48 hours. Speed depends on the country, embassy, and current availability.
Which Schengen countries do you cover?
+
We cover all 27 Schengen member states — France, Italy, Germany, Spain, Netherlands, Greece, Portugal, Austria, Belgium, Switzerland, Sweden, Norway, Denmark, Finland, Poland, Czech Republic, Estonia, Latvia, Lithuania, Luxembourg, Malta, Slovakia, Slovenia, Croatia, Iceland, Liechtenstein, and Hungary.
Is your service completely online?
+
Yes — 100% remote. All communication, document sharing, and payment happens via WhatsApp and email. You never need to visit any office. Get your appointment booked from the comfort of your home.
What documents do I need for a Schengen visa?
+
Core requirements include a valid passport, 2 passport photos, travel insurance (min. €30,000), confirmed flight itinerary, hotel booking, bank statements (3–6 months), and proof of income/employment. We provide a personalised checklist tailored to your specific country and visa type.
Do you provide dummy or confirmed flight bookings?
+
Yes — we provide both confirmed and dummy flight and hotel reservations that are fully accepted by Schengen embassies for visa application purposes.

We offer affordable packages with completely transparent pricing — no hidden fees. Our team will share exact pricing after your free initial consultation. Multiple secure payment options are available.
